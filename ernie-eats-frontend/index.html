About Us!

Our Mission

At Ernie's Eats, our mission is simple: to connect the people of Bridgewater with the incredible dining experiences right in their backyard. We're passionate about food, and we believe that every meal should be an adventure. Whether you're a local foodie or a visitor exploring our town, Ernie's Eats is here to guide you to the best restaurants, cafes, and eateries in the area.

User-Friendly Dining Discovery

Ernie's Eats is designed with you in mind. Our user-friendly platform allows you to effortlessly explore Bridgewater's dining scene, browse menus, read honest reviews, and find the perfect spot for your next meal. Whether you're craving a classic comfort food dish or want to try something entirely new, Ernie's Eats has got you covered.

Community-Driven Ratings

We believe that everyone's voice matters when it comes to dining choices. That's why we encourage you, our passionate community of food enthusiasts, to rate and review your dining experiences. Your feedback helps fellow foodies discover hidden gems and helps restaurants improve their offerings.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website.

What is the difference between a business account and a customer account?

Customer accounts can view, follow and review various restaurants while business accounts are strictly for restaurants. Business accounts also have more features including:

    Create posts for events or special announcements (e.g. closings, deals, etc.)
    Upload menus
    Upload hours
    Upload food images
