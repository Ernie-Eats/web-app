Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team through the Contact Us page on our website.
What is the difference between a business account and a customer account?
Customer accounts can view, follow and review various restaurants while business accounts are strictly for restaurants. Business accounts also have more features including: Create posts for events or special announcements
Upload menus
Upload hours
Upload food images
How do I post a review?
1. Create an account
2. Search for the restaurant
3. Select the restaurant
4. Write your review
5. Add Photos (optional)
6. Submit Your Review
What restaurants have reviews?
Restaurants in Bridgewater Virginia such as; The Cracked Pillar, El Charro, Sugar and Bean and more.
