#1

El Charro

El Charro's charm lies in its vibrant decor and savory dishes, blending traditional Mexican flavors with a familial touch. Patrons savor homemade tortillas and zesty salsas while basking in the warm hospitality. This cherished spot evokes nostalgia, creating lasting memories for generations in the heart of our community.

#2

The Cracked Pillar Pub

Craft Beer and great Burgers along with delicious Pub Fare ! Historic Hometown Hospitality are the Pillars in this family owned and operated Pub. The Cracked Pillar is a 200 year old National Historic Registry building ( The Barbee House) Great Beer Selection. Great Burgers and a best selling Reuben!

#3

Red Apple Restaurant

If you're craving some Chinese food in Bridgewater, then you've come to the right place! With dishes hailing from multiple Asian countries and prices that can't be beat, Red Apple Chinese Restaurant an outstanding Chinese restaurant.
