About

Ernie's Eats is a website where users can discover, share, and read reviews and recommendations about various restaurants in Bridgewater, Virginia. This website serves as a valuable resource for food enthusiasts, tourists, and Bridgewater college students.

Our Goal

We are a group of students from Bridgewater College with the goal of making a website designed to help both restaurants and customers. When our website is complete, businesses will be able to post to their own page and appear on our site for users to see. Customers can then review and see other reviews for restaurants in the area. Restaurants will also be able to post upcoming events they are having and have them featured on the front page.
