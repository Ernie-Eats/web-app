About Us!

Ernie's Eats is your go-to destination for exploring and celebrating the diverse culinary scene of Bridgewater, VA. Our platform, founded by a passionate group of Bridgewater College students, is dedicated to showcasing the local dining scene in our beloved community.

At Ernie's Eats, we provide a platform for both food enthusiasts and local restaurants to connect. Residents and visitors can leave their valuable ratings and reviews, while restaurants have the opportunity to share their menus, updates, upcoming events, and contact information. Join us in savoring the flavors of Bridgewater, one plate at a time.

Our Goals

  • Highlight Local Excellence: Our primary goal is to showcase the exceptional local restaurants in Bridgewater, VA. We believe that each establishment brings something special to our community, and we want to shine a spotlight on their hard work and creativity.

  • Empower Diners: We empower diners to be the critics and ambassadors of their local food scene. Ernie's Eats allows customers to share their experiences, leave ratings, and offer valuable feedback, helping others make informed dining choices.

  • Support Local Businesses: We aim to bolster the success of our local restaurants by providing them with a platform to post menus, share updates, promote special events, and offer contact information. We believe that a thriving culinary community benefits everyone.

  • Foster Connection: Ernie's Eats is all about building connections. We want to bring residents and visitors closer to their favorite restaurants and foster a sense of community around the shared love of great food.
